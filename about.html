Why should you trust Remote Start Reno with your vehicle electronics installation?
(Remote Start Reno versus “The Big Guys”)

Personal Responsibility
You are going to trust someone to crawl under your dashboard and work with your vehicles wiring.  Don’t you want to look that person in the eye and shake his hand?  I am personally responsible for your installation.  I will make sure that you know how to use your new system.  If you have a question or concern at a later date, you have my number.  This is my passion, my pride, my livelihood.  My one-man business lives and dies by word-of-mouth.  It is important to me that your unit is installed properly and continues to work properly long after you drive away.  I, Greg Tobin, will give you a clean professional installation, which isn’t something you can count on from an anonymous, hourly worker at a big shop.

Knowledge and Experience
I have Thirteen years of experience doing car electronics installations.  I was putting in car stereos and custom gauges before I even had a driver’s license.  I’ve worked on all kinds of vehicles, from basic old commuter cars to top of the line SUV’s, and sports cars.  This is an ever-changing industry and I strive to keep up with all of the new features and models.  I work closely with the unit manufacturers to keep up on technical data to ensure that I am up on all of the ins and outs, so that I can offer my customers the top-of-the line products and installation service.       

Warranty
Parts:  I get the same brand-new equipment with the same manufacturer’s warranty as the big guys.  I personally back that up with a 3-month warranty on all parts, so you don’t have to deal directly with the manufacturer in case of an equipment malfunction.  I only choose the most trusted brands, so I don’t expect you’ll ever have a problem.  If you do, I’ll make it right.
Service: I do it right the first time, so I gladly offer a lifetime warranty on my installation.  Simple as that.    

Convenience
At those big stores, you are an invoice number.  You drop off your vehicle at a time and place that may or may not be convenient to you.  You wait until the next available technician is available, while you try to coordinate a ride or just browse around, killing time.  At Remote Start Reno, you are a person.  You make an appointment, the unit will be prepped before you arrive, and most installations only take 1-2 hours max.   I have many evening and weekend appointments available for your convenience.

Price
Saving the best for last!  I don’t have a big shop with a ton of overhead, so I can pass those savings on to you.  I will do a better job and save you a substantial amount of money.  Additionally, I offer a choice of payment options, including cash, credit/debit cards and PayPal.
